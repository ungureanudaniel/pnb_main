{% trans 'If You See a Bear from a Distance' %}

  3. {% trans 'Do Not Approach' %}: {% trans 'Do not approach a bear for any reason.' %}
{% trans 'If the Bear Is Unaware of Your Presence' %}
  1. {% trans 'Retreat Slowly' %}: {% trans 'Try to retreat without drawing attention to yourself.' %}
  2. {% trans 'Avoid Sudden Movements and Loud Noises' %}: {% trans 'Slowly retreat and increase the distance.' %}
{% trans 'If the Bear Is Aware of Your Presence' %}
  1. {% trans 'Stay Calm' %}: {% trans 'Keep your movements slow and deliberate.' %}
  2. {% trans 'Identify Yourself' %}: {% trans 'Speak firmly to let it know you are human. When a bear stands on its hind legs, it is checking surroundings, not preparing to attack.' %}
  3. {% trans 'Do Not Run' %}: {% trans 'Running might trigger a chase.' %}
  4. {% trans 'Retreat Slowly' %}: {% trans 'Maintain eye contact and avoid turning your back on the bear.' %}
  5. {% trans 'Make Yourself Look Bigger' %}: {% trans 'Raise your arms, your backpack, open your jacket, and stand tall. If you are with others, stay together.' %}
{% trans 'If the Bear Approaches' %}
  1. {% trans 'Stand Your Ground' %}: {% trans 'Do not panic or run.' %}
  2. {% trans 'Make Noise' %}: {% trans 'Clap, shout, and make yourself look as intimidating as possible.' %}
  3. {% trans 'Drop Your Pack' %}: {% trans 'To distract the bear, drop items while retreating.' %}
  4. {% trans 'Use Bear Spray' %}: {% trans "If the bear is within 6-9 meters, use bear spray. Aim slightly downward, towards the bear's face, adjusting for wind." %}

  2. {% trans 'Use Bear Spray' %}: {% trans 'If the bear closes in, use bear spray.' %}
{% trans 'If the Bear Physically Attacks' %}
  1. {% trans 'Fight Back' %}: {% trans "Use any available objects to defend yourself. Aim for the eyes and nose." %}
{% trans 'General Preventive Measures' %}
  1. {% trans 'Travel in Groups' %}: {% trans 'Bears are less likely to approach larger groups.' %}

  3. {% trans 'Keep Dogs Leashed' %}: {% trans 'Dogs can provoke bears.' %}
  4. {% trans 'Use Marked Trails and Travel During the Day' %}: {% trans 'Stick to established paths and avoid hiking at night.' %}
  5. {% trans 'Avoid Attractants' %}: {% trans 'Properly store food and keep picnic/camping areas clean.' %}
  6. {% trans 'Do Not Try to Feed the Bears' %}
  7. {% trans "Do Not Stand in the Bear's Path" %}: {% trans 'If the bear is coming towards you, give it space to pass.' %}
